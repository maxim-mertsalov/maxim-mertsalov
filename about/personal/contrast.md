The Constant Learner

I’ve always believed that the best way to keep the mind sharp is to be a perpetual beginner. Currently, I’m exploring the worlds of piano and guitar. While I’m not ready to headline a concert hall just yet, I find a lot of joy in the "basics" — the slow progress of mastering a new chord or finally nailing a simple melody. For me, it’s about the rhythm of learning, not just the performance
