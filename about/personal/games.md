Atmospheric Adventures

When I’m not practicing scales or studying characters, you’ll likely find me immersed in beautifully crafted digital worlds. I have a major soft spot for games with deep atmospheres and intricate lore—specifically the haunting beauty of Hollow Knight and the gritty, post-apocalyptic storytelling of the Metro series (2033, Last Light, and Exodus). These games remind me of the importance of detail and atmosphere in any creative project
